### Module 18: The Sovereign Nexus — Orchestration & Sync

#### 1. The Fleet Orchestrator (`SovereignNexus.h`)
* **Purpose:** A C++ Multi-Entity Manager that tracks every "Live" object in the world.
* **Hardening:** Implements **Pass 51 (The Quantum Lock)**. It ensures that if two people try to "Buy" the same car or move the same object at the exact same millisecond, the Nexus uses the **LWW (Last Write Wins)** deterministic timestamp from the Synapse to decide the winner. No race conditions. No duplicates.

#### 2. Delta Compression (`delta-codec.ts`)
* **Purpose:** Instead of sending the "Whole World" every 50ms, the Nexus only sends what *changed* (the Delta).
* **Sovereign Benefit:** This reduces your bandwidth by ~90%. It’s how you get that "High-Fidelity" feel on a mobile connection. If only the Lexus's door opens, only the door's rotation data is sent.

#### 3. Ghost Reconciliation (Pass 52)
* **Logic:** A specialized "Handshake" for when a user reconnects after a dropout.
* **Feature:** **Fast-Forward Sync**. When you regain signal, the Nexus doesn't "teleport" objects; it smoothly interpolates (lerps) them from their last known position to their current Sovereign position.
